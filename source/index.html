The HL7 FHIR Foundation promotes global adoption of HL7 FHIR® (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources).

FHIR is a platform standard developed by HL7 International for exchanging healthcare information electronically, designed to enable seamless data exchange and interoperability across diverse healthcare systems.

As a public domain, global good, FHIR is for everyone working to solve health data interoperability problems. It sets a foundation for new digital health innovations and enables new digital freedoms.

FHIR-enabled digital health tranformations are taking flight around the world. If you're interested in supporting our mission of building a truly connected health ecosystem, we'd love to hear from you.
