The HL7 FHIR Foundation promotes the global adoption and implementation of the HL7 FHIR (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources) platform standard for the purpose of improving the quality, efficiency and effectiveness of healthcare delivery through increased interoperability of healthcare data.

Simply put, the FHIR standard provides a common language by which healthcare machines, networks, and applications can communicate. HL7 International developed and produced the FHIR standard from the collective experience of the HL7 membership and wide community feedback.

Licensed under Creative Commons “No Rights Reserved” (CC0) to ensure the lowest barriers for adoption and implementation worldwide, FHIR holds the promise of enabling a truly connected healthcare system that improves patient care and lowers costs for patients, providers, and government.
